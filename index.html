Simple Birthday Reminder 2.2

Simple Birthday Reminder is an Android app which keeps track of and reminds users of their loved ones birthdays.

Features

Remembering birthdays has never been so simple!

  • See a beautifully presented list of upcoming birthdays, each with the date, age and a countdown.
  • Simple to use, easy to see who is next and how long remains until each birthday.
  • Material design with many themes and colors to choose from.
  • Receive a reminder up to two weeks before the date, at the time of your choosing.
  • No adverts!
